Medical Information

This link leads to a Medicare Counseling website, which is extremely easy to use for users age of 65 and above, as it is a senior program. Counselors are trained in medicare and will help you understand insurance options to find the best for you. Learn more by attending community presentations or conferences hosted by counselors at HICAP. Said councelors provide one on one confidential counseling, assistance to seniors and family members, and legal assistance. This website also helps seniors with housing options, landlords, legal assistance, home modifications, memory problems, learning about hearing and visual impairments, transportation to appointments, and safe senior driving.
